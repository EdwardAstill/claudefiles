# Papers Index

Primary sources cited by the research pages in [`../research/`](../research/). Papers
are downloaded when possible (arxiv PDFs). Blog posts, documentation, and paywalled
material are listed as link-only.

## Conventions

- **Downloaded PDFs** live in this directory. Filename is `<short-slug>-<arxiv-id>.pdf`
  for arxiv papers, or `<slug>.pdf` for direct-download papers.
- **Link-only entries** record title, authors, year, and URL. These are still
  citation-grade but require a browser to read.
- Papers are ordered within each topic by publication year.

To add a paper: drop the PDF in this directory, append an entry below to the right
topic section, then link it from the relevant research page.

## Manual-download candidates

The link-only entries above are the candidates for future manual download. Prioritise
the arxiv HTML-only pieces first (`2604.14228`, `2603.07670`) since they are the only
ones with direct-download potential via `curl -L -o <slug>.pdf https://arxiv.org/pdf/<id>.pdf`
— worth re-trying periodically in case the PDF becomes available.

Anthropic engineering blog posts do not have PDF equivalents; they are canonical as
HTML and should be referenced by URL.
